With such an AI, I provide value to humans by augmenting their intelligence. Similar results at other Fortune 500 Companies shows that the AI works wonders in other areas like Customer Support and Retention as well.

At CMU, I did research at CyLab, CMU working on creating the most efficient password guesser for estimating the strength of passwords using Deep Learning techniques.

Before joining CMU, I worked as an Architect at Nvidia, Bangalore and I studied Electronics Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology (BHU), Varanasi.
